Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how youtubeNob ("App"), developed and maintained by {{ author_name }} ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account & Platform Credentials — OAuth tokens, API keys, client IDs and secrets you enter to connect third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram).
  • Content Data — Ideas, topics, scripts, images, audio files, and videos you create or upload within the App.
  • Settings & Preferences — Configuration options you set (e.g., default platform, GPU settings, font selection).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Platform Usernames & IDs — When you connect a third-party account (e.g., TikTok, Instagram), we store the username and platform-specific user ID returned by the platform's API.
  • Upload Metadata — Publish IDs, media IDs, video URLs, and upload statuses returned by third-party platforms after publishing.
  • Log Data — The App may generate local log files that contain timestamps, request paths, and error messages for debugging purposes.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal contact details (e-mail, phone number, physical address) unless you voluntarily provide them.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising pixels.
  • We do not share, sell, or rent your data to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate with and publish content to third-party platforms you have connected.
  • Generate, render, and manage video content as directed by you.
  • Store your settings, preferences, and content locally so the App functions correctly.
  • Debug and improve the App through locally stored log files.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your own infrastructure (your machine and your MongoDB instance). We do not operate remote servers that receive or store your data.
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored in your local MongoDB database. You are responsible for securing access to that database.
  • We recommend using strong passwords, firewall rules, and encryption to protect your local server and database.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party platforms. When you connect an account, data is transmitted directly between your local App instance and the platform's API:

  • YouTube / Google — Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok — TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta — Instagram Privacy Policy

Please review each platform's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

5. Data Retention

Your data remains stored locally for as long as you choose to keep it. You may delete any content, credentials, or settings at any time through the App's interface or by directly managing your MongoDB database. Disconnecting a third-party account removes the associated tokens from the database.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data stored about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Because all data is stored locally on your own infrastructure, you have direct and full control over exercising these rights at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via LinkedIn.
